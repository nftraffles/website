How Do NFT Raffles Work?


We use Non Fungible Tokens minted on the blockchain to function as digital raffle tickets for art, collectibles, and other rare objects.

Collectors can go to marketplaces which support the ethereum nft standard to buy and trade our raffle tickets. The raffle event is generated after all available tickets are purchased, and the address with the winning raffle ticket will recieve a unique NFT prize which will contain instructions on receiving the original work or collectible.

The address that is holding the winning raffle ticket(s) during the time of purchase of the last available raffle ticket will be the winner.

Turning Raffle Tickets Into Collectible Items


The raffle ticket itself can become tradable assets due to the nature of Non Fungible Tokens. After the raffle event has proceeded, the holder of the raffle ticket is free to sell the ticket at any price that might find a buyer.

This creates a potential scenario for collectors to hedge against the cost of the raffle entry, by being able to arbitrate the value of the raffle ticket digital asset.
